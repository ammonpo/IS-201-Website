Education

This is what I've learned

I've learned countless things in my educational career that have helped me develop my knowledge, but more importantly have helped me grow personally. It began all the way back from when I was a preshcool dropout. Walking into that group, in a foreign country, I wondered if the task ahead was too daunting. It was too much, I quit. This was not the end, however. My comeback to Kindergarten was impactful. It set the stage and layed the groundwork for all that I know today.

The story continues as I enrolled to become a student in an immersion school. Half my days were spent learning the ancient language of Chinese. This helped me develop in my knowledge of culture and respect, alongside my knowledge of strokes and radicals, tones and pinyin.

My next chapter is being written as I continue my education at Brigham Young University. Here I learn that we, "enter to learn, go forth to serve." But this will not be my final chapter. This is but the beginning of a life of learning. Oh the joy this brings.
